Argentina Independence Developement

The first attempt for become a free country was on May 25th of 1810. News were coming from Spain saying that Fernando VII, king of Spain, was captured by Napoleón Bonaparte. This situation leave the colonies vulnerable for be claimed by other Kingdoms. People from Buenos Aires decided to act opening the Cabildo for take a decision. The delicate situation turn the act revolutionary and the people from Buenos Aires claimed for the administration of the Viceroyaltyand, the Viceking had to let and was conformed the first government compose for spanish and criollos (Sons of the Spanish migrators who were born in America). The name of that assembly was Primera Junta (First Assembly). The people who were part of the assembly:

This were the most important decisions that the Primera Junta took:

  • Remove the Virrey from his charge, and established the Primera Junta as the administrator of the Viceroyalty of the Rio de la Plata.
  • More liberty for commerce, new ports were allowed to commertial activities.
  • Creation of armies.
  • Expeditions to the frontier looking for productive lands
  • Control of the mines in Upper Perú province
